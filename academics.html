Academic Programs

Nurturing minds from early childhood to higher secondary.

Pre-Primary Foundation

Our Pre-Primary program focuses on an active, play-based learning approach to nurture innate curiosity. We ensure kids develop foundational literacy and basic arithmetic in a loving, inclusive environment.

  • Focus on sensory and motor skill development
  • Interactive storytelling and rhymes
  • Social skills and emotional intelligence

Primary & Middle (Std 1-8)

Building on foundational curiosity, our primary and middle school curriculum introduces structured learning across sciences, languages, and humanities to develop critical thinkers.

  • Comprehensive learning in Mathematics, Science and English
  • Introduction to regional languages and culture
  • Project-based learning to apply textbook concepts to real life
